# Vector's Vision: OpenCV with Vector
---
# The idea
Vector has a camera that is exposed to the python SDK, and can be accesed with `robot.camera.init_camera_feed()`. This can make him do things like:
- Play Rock Paper Scicors
- Interact and follow objects other then the cube (laser pointer anyone?)
- Guard your snacks
---
# What he CAN'T do:
- Use OpenCV in wire-pod
- Take over the world
